Post-quantum cryptography planning

Current RSA and ECC encryption will eventually be broken by quantum computers. NIST finalized the first post-quantum standards (ML-KEM, ML-DSA) in 2024. Ryan assesses your cryptographic posture and builds a migration roadmap before you're vulnerable — not after.

Algorithmic network analysis

Graph-based topology mapping, traffic pattern baselines, and anomaly detection algorithms that surface threats before they become incidents. Most small organizations have no visibility into what's normal on their network — this fixes that.

Zero-trust architecture

Never trust, always verify. Zero-trust means no implicit access — every user, device, and connection is authenticated and authorized at every step. Ryan designs and implements zero-trust frameworks scaled to what your organization actually needs.

AI-augmented operations

AI tools are most valuable when they're integrated into how operations actually work — not added as a layer on top. Ryan builds AI-augmented workflows for monitoring, incident triage, documentation, and pattern recognition that reduce manual overhead and sharpen response time.
